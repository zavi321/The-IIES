Introduction

Where Faith Meets Modern Learning

The Iqra Islamic Educational System Puran is committed to providing students with a balanced combination of modern education, Islamic values, moral training, and character development — nurturing a generation that excels academically while remaining firmly connected to Islamic teachings.

By choosing the name Iqra, the institution emphasizes that education is not merely about passing examinations but about developing responsible, knowledgeable, and God-conscious individuals — where discipline, respect, and faith go hand in hand.

The school's vision extends beyond the classroom, helping students build strong communication skills, critical thinking, leadership, confidence, and a sense of responsibility toward society, family, and community.

Our Journey

Our History

The Iqra Islamic Educational System (The IIES) was established in 2018 with the great vision of providing the children of the region with a quality educational environment — one where Islamic education, high moral character, and modern knowledge come together in perfect harmony.

The institution was founded by three sincere, capable, and education-loving individuals: Engineer Imdad Ullah Momin, Engineer Subhan Ullah, and Maulana Muhammad Hussain. Their goal was not merely to establish a school, but to raise a generation adorned with knowledge, character, faith, and morality — one capable of serving their society and country in the best possible way.

The institution began its educational journey with the Nursery class. By the grace of Allah Almighty, the visionary leadership of its founders, the tireless efforts of its teachers, and the full trust of parents, the institution achieved new milestones of progress every year. New classes were added step by step, until today, Alhamdulillah, The Iqra Islamic Educational System (The IIES) has successfully reached up to Class 8.

Our effort is to provide students with an education that makes them successful in academic, moral, and practical fields. In sha Allah, the institution will continue its educational journey toward higher classes in the future, while further raising its academic standards.

"Knowledge, character, and faith — this is our identity, our asset, and our commitment."

Discipline

Students are expected to arrive on time, respect teachers and classmates, maintain cleanliness, wear proper uniforms, complete homework regularly, and follow school rules — habits that prepare them to become responsible individuals both inside and outside school.

Parental Partnership

Education becomes more successful when parents and teachers work together. Regular communication enables parents to monitor academic progress, support learning at home, and celebrate their child's achievements alongside the school.
